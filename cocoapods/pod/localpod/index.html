LocalPod

A LocalPod interfaces one or more specifications belonging to one pod (a library) and their concrete instance in the file system.

The LocalPod is responsible for orchestrating the activated specifications of a single pod. Specifically, it keeps track of the activated specifications and handles issues related to duplicates files. Inheritance logic belongs to the Specification class.

The activated specifications are used to compute the paths that can be safely cleaned by the pod.
